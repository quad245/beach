Looking for a beach resort?

There’s nothing quite like waking up to the sound of swirling waves and the smell of sea air through your bedroom window. Beachfront accommodation comes in a variety of forms, from well decked-out resorts to secluded homestays and villas. Beach hotel amenities can include air-conditioned rooms, private terraces overlooking the sea and outdoor pools with adjacent bars.

Moonshine

Moonshine B&B is located in Lagos, 600 meters from the Marina of Lagos and 400 meters from Meia Praia (the largest beach in Lagos). Our facility is located in a very quiet area (reachable through a small stretch of white road), where you can relax in a peaceful and tranquil environment. Moonshine is a small family-run business consisting of 4 studios with separate entrances and a completely separate apartment, a large swimming pool (open 24 / 24h), a relaxation area with deck chairs and a bbq area. All the accommodations have private bathrooms and private kitchenettes. The rooms are refreshed (change of towels and sheets) for half the stay for those staying more than a week. To welcome you and welcome you to the Moonshine B&B you will find an Italian family composed of: Michele (Italian chef), his wife Stefania and his family. Our goal is to make you enjoy a relaxing and peaceful holiday, immersed in one of the most beautiful areas of Lagos. We do our best to leave space and privacy for our guests, but we are still available for any information or need. Languages spoken: English,French,Italian
