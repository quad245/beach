Lagos Beaches

It is located at Victoria Island, Lagos. Bar Beach, has served a variety of purposes in the History of Nigeria. During the military regime in the 1970s and 1980s, the bar beach served as a public execution centre of the Nigerian Army Firing Squad. Convicted persons were led to the beach where they were executed.

Moist Beach Club

Moist beach is one of the best beaches in Lagos. It has become an entertainment hub with a restaurant, lounge with nightlife experience. It has both an open view and an indoor experience. Furthermore, it is located in the Victoria Island area of Lagos. Moist beach is an exclusive premium beach and a perfect relaxation centre. Moist Beach club is located inside Oniru Private Beach.

Elegushi Beach

The Elegushi Royal Private beach offers unending music, exotic local delicacies, foods and amazing ocean view, a perfect spot for social events, birthday parties, picnics, hangouts, reunions etc The Elegushi Private beach is located around Ikate, Lekki Area of Lagos Nigeria. Elegushi is one of the most interesting and fun beaches in Lagos that never sleeps, The beach operates almost 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and 365 days a year and this is one of the most lively beaches in Lagos. Because Elegushi doesn’t sleep it offers a variety of nightclubs, bars, lounges and restaurants and don’t get carried away this beach has a very strong waves caution must be observed.

Oniru Private Beach

The Oniru Private Beach is one of the popular beaches in Lagos It is a beautiful and serene beach owned and managed by the Oniru Royal Family. It is quiet and less noisy compared to other beaches in Lagos is one of the beautiful and serene private beaches in Lagos. Oniru beach is highly rated for its cleanliness and it is also a well-secured waterfront beach. It is a peaceful, neat and located in a lovely atmosphere.

Shiro/Hard Rock cafe beach

The Hard Rock café is an exquisite top of the range restaurant with a beautiful ocean view. The Hard Rock Café may not be one of the most famous beaches in Lagos, but it is definitely a haven for tourist and fun seekers. The Shiro/Hardrock Café shares a border with the Oniru Beach. There are lots of activities going on at the Oniru beach which includes live music, games, horse riding etc. It is also one of the popular beaches in Lagos. This beach is currently not in existence and has become part of the Landmark beach

Chaka Resort

Chaka Resort is also known as Chakaville Beach Resort. It is not one of the popular beaches in Lagos but it offers beautiful beachfront views and definitely one of the best beach resorts in Lagos. It comes equipped with lots of recreational activities, tourist attractions and games for both kids and adults.

Fofina Bamboo Beach Resort

Fofina Bamboo Beach Resort is a privately owned beach resort aimed ar providing, a quality relaxation centre for everyone lounge, fun spaces and superb facilities. It also offers music, beautiful scenery, beach games like volleyball, badminton, swimming etc.
